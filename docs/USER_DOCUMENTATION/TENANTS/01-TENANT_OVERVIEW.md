## **User Roles & Permissions**

### **üîê Tenant User Roles**

#### **üë§ Admin**
- **Full Business Control**: Complete access to all business functions
- **Permissions**: 
  - Manage users and roles
  - Configure business settings
  - Access all business data (customers, products, orders, vendors)
  - View analytics and reports
  - Manage integrations and customizations

#### **üë§ Manager** 
- **Operations Management**: Business operations without user management
- **Permissions**:
  - Manage customers, products, orders, vendors
  - View business analytics
  - Process orders and handle customer service
  - Cannot manage users or business settings

#### **üë§ Sales**
- **Customer & Order Focus**: Customer relationship and order management
- **Permissions**:
  - Manage customers and their information
  - Process and manage orders
  - View relevant analytics (sales performance)
  - Cannot manage products or vendors

#### **üë§ Viewer**
- **Read-Only Access**: View business data without modification rights
- **Permissions**:
  - View customers, products, orders
  - Access analytics and reports
  - Cannot modify any business data
