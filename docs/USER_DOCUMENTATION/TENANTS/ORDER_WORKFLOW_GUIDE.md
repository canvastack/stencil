# Order Workflow Management Guide

## Overview

This comprehensive guide covers the complete order workflow management system for PT Custom Etching Xenial (PT CEX) business model. The system is designed to handle the complex intermediary (makelar) business process from customer inquiry to final delivery.

## Business Model Context

PT CEX operates as an intermediary connecting customers with specialized vendors for custom etching products. The workflow is designed to:

- **Manage Customer Relationships**: Handle inquiries, quotations, and communications
- **Vendor Coordination**: Source, negotiate, and manage vendor relationships
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensure product quality meets customer expectations
- **Financial Management**: Handle payments, margins, and vendor settlements

## Order Lifecycle Stages

### 1. Draft Stage (Pesanan Baru)

**Purpose**: Initial order review and validation

**Key Activities**:
- Review customer specifications
- Validate contact information
- Ensure all required details are complete
- Document special requirements

**Business Rules**:
- All specifications must be complete before proceeding
- Customer contact must be verified
- Special requirements must be documented

**Common Issues**:
- Incomplete specifications
- Invalid customer contact
- Unclear requirements

**Best Practices**:
- Contact customer immediately for clarifications
- Document all communications
- Use standardized specification templates

### 2. Pending Stage (Review Admin)

**Purpose**: Administrative approval and preparation for vendor sourcing

**Key Activities**:
- Final administrative review
- Prepare for vendor sourcing
- Set up project tracking

**Business Rules**:
- Admin approval required before vendor sourcing
- All customer information must be validated
- Project timeline must be established

### 3. Vendor Sourcing (Pencarian Vendor)

**Purpose**: Find and evaluate suitable vendors

**Key Activities**:
- Identify potential vendors
- Send Request for Quotation (RFQ)
- Evaluate vendor responses
- Create vendor shortlist

**Business Rules**:
- **CRITICAL**: Minimum 3 vendors must be contacted
- Vendors must meet quality standards
- Response timeline: 48 hours maximum
- All communications must be documented

**Common Issues**:
- Vendors not responding to RFQ
- Prices too high from all vendors
- Vendor capacity insufficient
- Quality standards not met

**Best Practices**:
- Prepare detailed and clear RFQ
- Set realistic response deadlines
- Check vendor track record and references
- Consider geographic location for shipping efficiency
- Maintain vendor database with performance history

**Documentation Required**:
- RFQ templates
- Vendor evaluation criteria
- Response comparison matrix
- Vendor qualification checklist

### 4. Vendor Negotiation (Negosiasi Vendor)

**Purpose**: Negotiate terms with selected vendors

**Key Activities**:
- Price negotiation
- Timeline discussion
- Quality standards agreement
- Payment terms finalization

**Business Rules**:
- **CRITICAL**: All agreements must be documented in writing
- **CRITICAL**: Price comparison from minimum 2 vendors required
- Quality guarantee must be obtained
- Penalty clauses for delays must be included

**Common Issues**:
- Vendor unwilling to reduce prices
- Timeline too long for customer needs
- Payment terms not acceptable
- Quality standards disagreement

**Best Practices**:
- Document all negotiations
- Request quality guarantees
- Discuss penalty clauses for delays
- Ensure vendor understands specifications clearly
- Get legal review for complex contracts

**Documentation Required**:
- Negotiation records
- Price comparison sheets
- Contract templates
- Quality guarantee documents

### 5. Customer Quote (Quote ke Customer)

**Purpose**: Prepare and send quotation to customer

**Key Activities**:
- Calculate pricing with appropriate margin
- Prepare detailed quotation
- Include terms and conditions
- Send to customer

**Business Rules**:
- Margin calculation must be approved by management
- Quote must include detailed breakdown
- Terms and conditions must be included
- Quote validity period must be specified

**Best Practices**:
- Provide clear price breakdown
- Include timeline and delivery terms
- Offer flexible payment options
- Include terms and conditions
- Set reasonable quote validity period

### 6. Awaiting Payment (Menunggu Pembayaran)

**Purpose**: Follow up on customer payment

**Key Activities**:
- Follow up with customer
- Prepare invoices
- Track payment status
- Prepare contracts

**Business Rules**:
- Follow up within 2-3 days after quote sent
- Payment terms must be clearly communicated
- Invoice must be prepared upon agreement

**Best Practices**:
- Regular follow-up schedule
- Provide easy payment options
- Prepare contracts for signing
- Document all customer communications

### 7. Payment Received (Pembayaran Diterima)

**Purpose**: Process payment and initiate production

**Key Activities**:
- Verify payment receipt
- Notify vendor to start production
- Create purchase orders
- Set up production tracking

**Business Rules**:
- Payment verification within 24 hours
- Vendor notification immediately after payment confirmation
- Purchase order must be created for vendor

**Best Practices**:
- Confirm payment quickly
- Send thank you message to customer
- Ensure vendor is ready to start production
- Set up production monitoring system

### 8. In Production (Dalam Produksi)

**Purpose**: Monitor production progress

**Key Activities**:
- Regular progress monitoring
- Quality checks
- Customer updates
- Issue resolution

**Business Rules**:
- Regular communication with vendor required
- Quality checks at predetermined milestones
- Customer updates at agreed intervals

**Common Issues**:
- Production delays
- Quality issues
- Vendor communication problems
- Specification changes

**Best Practices**:
- Daily communication with vendor
- Request progress photos
- Maintain quality control checklist
- Provide regular customer updates
- Have backup plans for issues

### 9. Quality Control (Quality Control)

**Purpose**: Final quality inspection before delivery

**Key Activities**:
- Comprehensive product inspection
- Documentation of quality check
- Approval or rejection decision
- Issue resolution if needed

**Business Rules**:
- **CRITICAL**: All products must pass quality inspection
- Quality check must be documented with photos
- Rejection must be communicated immediately to vendor

**Common Issues**:
- Product doesn't meet specifications
- Finishing quality issues
- Dimension inaccuracies
- Material defects

**Best Practices**:
- Use comprehensive quality checklist
- Photo document all aspects
- Immediate communication for issues
- Have clear rejection/rework procedures

### 10. Shipping (Pengiriman)

**Purpose**: Arrange and monitor product delivery

**Key Activities**:
- Coordinate with shipping provider
- Ensure proper packaging
- Provide tracking information
- Monitor delivery progress

**Business Rules**:
- Reliable shipping provider must be used
- Tracking number must be provided to customer
- Insurance required for high-value items

**Best Practices**:
- Choose reliable and trackable couriers
- Ensure secure packaging
- Provide tracking information immediately
- Prepare customer service for delivery questions

### 11. Completed (Selesai)

**Purpose**: Finalize order and gather feedback

**Key Activities**:
- Confirm customer receipt
- Collect feedback
- Close order
- Update vendor performance records

**Best Practices**:
- Follow up within 1-2 days after delivery
- Request reviews or testimonials
- Document lessons learned
- Update vendor performance database

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Vendor-Related Issues

**Issue**: Vendor not responding to RFQ
**Solution**: 
- Set clear response deadlines
- Follow up with phone calls
- Have backup vendor list
- Consider removing non-responsive vendors

**Issue**: All vendor prices too high
**Solution**:
- Review specifications for cost optimization
- Negotiate volume discounts
- Consider alternative materials or processes
- Expand vendor search geographically

### Customer-Related Issues

**Issue**: Customer not responding to quote
**Solution**:
- Follow up with different communication channels
- Offer payment plan options
- Review and adjust pricing if necessary
- Set quote expiration dates

**Issue**: Customer requesting changes during production
**Solution**:
- Assess impact on timeline and cost
- Communicate additional charges clearly
- Get written approval for changes
- Update vendor immediately

### Quality Issues

**Issue**: Product fails quality control
**Solution**:
- Document issues with photos
- Communicate immediately with vendor
- Determine if rework is possible
- Have clear rework/replacement procedures
